Token


Turtle Tribe will be releasing a deflationary token called $TRIBLING. This token will have many deflationary mechanisms to keep it healthy. Firstly, buying and selling fees whenever someone buys or sells our token a fee of 10% will be deducted from the transaction and this 10% will be distributed as follows 5% will be burned forever, 3% will be added as liquidity, and 2% will go to the devs. This is done to make the Turtle Tribe an everlasting community!

Distribution


$TRIBLING will have a total supply of 1 million (Note to team: maybe less still got to do a few calculations to find the ideal amount) and 75% of the supply will be locked in a reward pool to then be earned through staking, 10% will be publicly deployed as an IDO and 15% will be airdropped to people who minted a TT NFT before 20% was minted!
