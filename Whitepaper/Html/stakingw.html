Staking


This feature was mainly added to be able to distribute our coin $TRIBLING to our loyal community who decide to hold our NFT’s. In addition, it allows people who bought our NFT’s to get a return on their investment in an easy and safe way. However, to protect our coin from inflation there will be a limited time of one month where holders of TT NFT can earn $TRIBLING after this one month any coins left in the reward pool will be burned forever.

Distribution of Rewards


To make it fair for everyone and to help give back to our community we will be implementing a multiplier for staking. This multiplier will take effect if you stake an NFT of higher rarity which would then cause your returns to multiply and you will earn more $TRIBLING than if you staked regular NFT's. This is done so that the Turtle Tribe NFT's that are more rare have a much greater value than the common ones.
