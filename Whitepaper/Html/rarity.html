NFT Rarities


TurtleTribe NFTs will come in rarities, which will bring forth greater earning opportunities to game modes in TurtleCove. Head to the Rarity section in our game to get more details. During minting, a roll will be made and you'll be assigned a random rarity from common to rare, extravagant and legendary depending on your role. Drop rates are as follows:
Common: 70%
Rare: 20%
Extravagant: 8%
Legendary: 2%
