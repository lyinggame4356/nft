How we are helping the environment


Here at Turtle Tribe we believe in giving back and helping others. To do so we have decided to create a passive income opportunity for players as mentioned later on. However, we are also devoted to helping out the environment, especially the ocean life that we as humans have destroyed. That is why we are glad to announce that 15% of our income from this project will go to a select charity from reef.org. In addition, we have decided that our project will mainly be focused on a staking mechanism further increasing our positive influence on the environment by not demanding the use of miners!
